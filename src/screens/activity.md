# The Activity screen

Who did what, newest first. Every budget assignment, money move, goal change,
categorization, and membership change — with the member's name and when.

Shared money fails on mystery. "Who moved the grocery money?" is a fight.
This page makes it a fact instead: someone did, it's right there, and they had
a reason. Ask about the reason, not the deed.

Entries can't be edited or deleted — by anyone. A log you can rewrite isn't a
log.

There's nothing to configure here. Read it when something surprises you, and
skim it monthly even when nothing does.
