# Write plain, count honest.

<p class="lede">SkrillaPlan is envelope budgeting for your household. Money
comes in, you give every dollar a job, you spend from the envelopes. The app
keeps the envelopes honest.</p>

<a class="cta" href="/getting-started/">Start here — set it up in an evening →</a>

## What it is

An old system with new plumbing. Your banks feed transactions in every
morning (or you import them yourself — both are real budgeting). You sort
them into categories in your own words. You assign every dollar a job. When
plans meet reality, you move money instead of giving up.

Your whole household works from the same budget, live, with an honest log of
who changed what. No mystery, no fights about mystery.

## What it costs

One plan, everything in it: **$9.99/mo** or **$79/yr**. No tiers, no
feature ransom. Free during alpha. The details are at
[skrillaplan.com/pricing](https://skrillaplan.com/pricing) — and whatever
happens with your subscription, you can always see and export every byte of
your data. Nobody is ever locked out of their own numbers.

## Privacy is the product

Your money is your business. SkrillaPlan has no ads, no data selling, no
analytics scripts watching you type. Bank sign-in happens inside Plaid; your
password never touches us. Even this help site loads nothing from anyone
else — check the network tab, we'll wait.

The [privacy policy](https://skrillaplan.com/privacy) takes five minutes to
read. That's on purpose. The even shorter version lives here:
[What we don't do](/what-we-dont-do/).
