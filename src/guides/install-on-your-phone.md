# Put SkrillaPlan on your phone

The budget you check is the budget that works. And you check things on your
phone. Two taps put SkrillaPlan on your home screen — real icon, full
screen, no app store, nothing to download.

## iPhone

1. Open **skrillaplan.com** in Safari.
2. Tap the **Share** button — the square with the arrow pointing up.
3. Tap **Add to Home Screen**, then **Add**.

## Android

1. Open **skrillaplan.com** in Chrome.
2. Tap the **⋮** menu in the corner.
3. Tap **Add to Home screen** (or **Install app** — Chrome sometimes offers
   an install banner and that works too).

The Skrilla icon lands on your home screen. Done.

## What you get

- **Its own window.** Opens full screen like any app — no address bar, no
  browser chrome. Notch-safe, no zoom-jumping when you tap a field.
- **Navigation at thumb height.** On a phone the tabs live in a bar along
  the bottom: **Budget · Transactions · Reports · ⋯ More**. **More** opens
  a small menu for Categories, Banks, and Account. The tab you're on glows.
  The top of the screen keeps the small stuff — logo, ☀️/🌙, sign out.
- **Always current.** It's the live app, not a copy. No updates to install,
  no version to fall behind. Open it and you're on the newest SkrillaPlan,
  every time.

## One honest limit

It needs a connection. Offline, the app says so instead of showing you
yesterday's numbers dressed up as today's. We won't guess at your
money.

Native apps for the app stores are coming. Until they do, this is the
mobile app — and it never needs an update.
