# Signing in and passwords

Two ways through the front door at **skrillaplan.com/login**. Both end in
the same budget.

## The Google button

One click, nothing to remember. If your invite went to a Gmail address,
this is the easy path.

## Email and password

1. Choose **or with email** and create your account.
2. Check your inbox and click the verification link we send. It isn't
   busywork — it proves you own the address your invite went to.
3. Sign in.

Password rules, all of them: **six characters minimum, longer is better.**
No uppercase-symbol-number theater — length is what protects you.

## Forgot your password?

**Forgot password?** on the sign-in page emails you a reset link. You can
always get back in on your own.

## One account per email

An email address has one SkrillaPlan account. If you started with the
Google button, that *is* your account — the sign-in page points you back
to it rather than let you make a confused twin.

## Two footnotes

- **Invites match on email.** Whichever way you sign in, use the address
  the invite went to — the match is exact.
- **Receipt matching still wants Gmail.** Email sign-in changes nothing
  there: [receipt matching](/guides/receipt-matching/) works with a
  connected Gmail mailbox only, for now.
