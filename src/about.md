# Who's behind SkrillaPlan

SkrillaPlan is made by **Skrilla Plan, LLC** — a small shop, not a
venture-backed data machine. The difference shows up in the product.

## Funded by you, on purpose

Most money apps are free because *you* are the product — your spending
habits packaged and sold, your screen quietly recorded, ads slipped between
your own transactions. We took the other deal: you pay a fair price, and
that price is the whole business model.

**$9.99 a month, or $79 a year.** That's what keeps the lights on. Not ads,
not data sales, not "trusted partners." When the people who use the app are
the people who pay for it, the app can afford to be on their side.

That's why we can promise no trackers, no ads, no selling — and mean it.
It's not restraint. It's arithmetic. We don't need your data, so we don't
take it. [Here's the full list of what we don't do](/what-we-dont-do/).

## What we're building

A budget you'll actually keep. Honest numbers, plain words, and a tool that
respects both your money and your privacy — especially if you've never
budgeted before. That's the whole mission. An old typewriter and a money
clip on the same desk: write plain, count honest.

We're in alpha, building in the open, and every rough edge you report makes
it better. Thanks for being early. *→ [Welcome to the alpha](/alpha/)*
