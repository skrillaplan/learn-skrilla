# Join the community

Budgeting alone works. Budgeting with company works better. The SkrillaPlan
community lives at **forum.skrillaplan.com** — people running real budgets,
comparing notes out loud.

We call it a mastermind, and here's the honest definition: people at the
same table, showing their work. Wins, questions, money talk. Nobody at the
head of the table selling anything.

## Invite-only, on purpose

Every member was let in by a person. That's curation, not exclusion — a
small room where people talk about money has to be a room you can trust.

Invites go to alpha users first. Want in? Say so at
**hello@skrillaplan.com**, or through the 🐛 button in the app.

## Your forum account

When your invite arrives, you'll make a forum account — quick, and for now
it's separate from your app sign-in. Two doors, one house.

## The house rules

Four, and they're short:

- **Be generous.** Post so the next person who searches finds an answer.
- **No selling.** Nothing has a price tag in this room.
- **No financial-advice-as-a-service.** Compare notes; don't hang a
  shingle.
- **Never share someone else's numbers outside the room.** What's posted
  here stays here.

How the room stays this way: [How moderation works](/community/moderation/).
How to make your posts land: [Posting well](/community/posting-well/).

## Privacy

The forum runs on our own servers. No trackers, no ads, no analytics
scripts watching you type. Your posts belong to the room — and the room
belongs to the people in it.
